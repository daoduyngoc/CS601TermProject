Countries I have been to

Japan

Japan is where I have met the most kind and polite people. The country has more than 2000 years of history has gone through many up and down, and the Japanese are proud of that. Blending with the modern and advanced technology, the long history culture still remains intact with its own elegance and beauty.

Capital: Tokyo
Population: 125.8 million (2020)
Currency: Japanese Yen (¥)

Malaysia

Malaysia is a rich cultured country with friendly and hardworking people. The capital city, Kuala Lumpur, has many skyscrapers and a modern monorail system. I was blown away by how clean and developed the city is, even though it is just couple of hours of flight from hometown. The country is on its course to achieve high income status between 2024 and 208 according to World Bank.

Capital: Kuala Lumpur
Population: 32.37 million (2020)
Currency: Malaysian Ringgit (RM)

Mexico

Mexico - our neighbor down south is a beautiful country. This is my most recent trip after the pandemic. Going from the cold and rainy Seattle to the warmer weather and people of Mexico was a pleasant change of pace. Everything about this country made me want to revisit it again in the future.

Capital: Mexico City
Population: 128.9 million (2020)
Currency: Mexican Peso (₱)
